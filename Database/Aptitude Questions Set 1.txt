1. If a person walks at 14 km/hr instead of 10 km/hr, he would have walked 20 km more. The actual distance travelled by him is:
50 km
56 km
70 km
80 km

Correct Answer: 70 km
Explanation: (14−10)t = 20 ⇒ t = 5 ⇒ Distance = 14×5
